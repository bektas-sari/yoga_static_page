My Background

I am a certified yoga instructor with over 10 years of experience in teaching various styles of yoga. My journey began with a passion for wellness and a desire to help others find balance in their lives.

Philosophy

I believe in the holistic approach to yoga, integrating mind, body, and spirit. My classes focus on mindfulness, breath control, and the importance of self-care.

Qualifications

I hold a 500-hour Yoga Teacher Training certification and have completed various workshops in specialized yoga practices. I am committed to continuous learning and growth in my practice.
